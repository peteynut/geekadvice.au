A round-up of what's changed in the Race Wars physics model this month. The big one is the
suspension — we finally have a MacPherson strut setup that doesn't bottom out at speed.

## What changed

- Re-tuned spring rates per axle.
- Added a small amount of negative camber gain under compression.
- Replaced the magic dampening constant with a derived one from spring rate.

```ts
// derived damping coefficient
const damping = springRate * 0.35;
```

Next up: the tire model. Currently it's pure lateral-grip saturation, which is fine for arcade
but causes weird behavior on kerbs. Plan is a simplified Pacjeka curve.
