Mostly small fixes this time around. The headline item is the new replay camera, which actually
behaves like you'd expect now.

## Highlights

- Replay camera now follows the player car, not the last spawned one.
- Fixed a crash when a player disconnected during the podium cinematic.
- The shop now remembers your last selected paint when you come back to the garage.

## Known issues

We're still tracking a weird GPU crash on certain Intel Macs in the rain. Workaround for now is
to drop rain density to 0 in settings.
